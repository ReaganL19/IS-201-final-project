Cheesy Bread


How to make cheesy bread:

  1. preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. gather ingredients:
    • 1 cup olive oil
    • 1 cup whole milk
    • 3 eggs
    • 1 cup gouda
    • 1/2 cup white cheddar
    • 1/2 cup parmesean
    • 3 cups tapioca flour
    • 2 tsp salt
  3. blend ingredients together
  4. pour batter in muffin tins
  5. bake for 15-20 minutes @ 400

Why cheesy bread is so good

This cheesy bread is amazing if you cannot have gluten as tapioca flour is gluten free. Thsi bread is also great when you want carbs but need a little more nutrients. It is also great it you love cheese like me. It's super easy to make and tastes great right out of the oven, or reheated in the microwave for a few seconds. It is one of my favorite snacks and would also be great as an alternative to dinner rolls.

Careful! you may end up eating a whole pan in one night!
